Visit the studio

You are cordially invited to visit our new working studio and gallery. for our special holiday open house! Friday & Saturday 11-8 p.m. and Sunday 1-5 p.m. Nov. 26-28 and Dec. 3-5.

Open most other days 1 to 5 p.m. Will open for appointments as well.

Location: The Mudcat studio is located at 301 Jefferson St. in historic downtown Madison, Indiana (the most beautiful rivertown in America if you haven't heard).

You'll find easy parking right across the street!
[ Get a map ]

Our studio is just two blocks from where the riverboats dock on the Ohio River. The 160-year-old three-story commercial building has been a coffeehouse, a hotel, tenements and a feed store. We look forward to continuing its interesting history.

Occasionally business pulls us out of town. If you're planning on stopping in town feel free to call or email ahead to double-check we'll be open. (812) 265-2445 or darlene@mudcatpottery.com

Vacation: We will be on vacation visiting family Nov. 12-20. Sorry , if we'll miss you!
