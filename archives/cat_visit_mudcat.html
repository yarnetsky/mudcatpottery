Find us at the Chautauqua

Saturday and Sunday
September 24 & 25

Darlene has new pots coming out of the kiln daily in preparation for this weekend's Madison Chautauqua. Our booth is #614 located in front of the Lanier Mansion on the riverfront's Vaughn Drive.

Come visit us!!! Thoughout the fall, our studio will be open most afternoons 1 to 5 p.m.

Visit the studio

Our studio is located just two blocks from where the riverboats dock on the Ohio River. The 160-year-old three-story commercial building has been a coffeehouse, a hotel, tenements and a feed store. We look forward to continuing its interesting history.

Darlene's studio is open most days 1 to 5 p.m. Will open for appointments as well.

Location: The Mudcat studio is located at 301 Jefferson St. in historic downtown Madison, Indiana (the most beautiful rivertown in America if you haven't heard).

You'll find easy parking right across the street!
