{Shipping pots}

The trick to shipping pots in the mail is to double box them. Wrap the pot in bubble wrap and place in a box slightly larger than the wrapped pot. Fill in the dead spaces with styrofoam peanuts. Then suspend this box in a larger box with a couple inches width of peanuts on all four sides of the box. We ship U.S. Mail as UPS charges a lot more for oversized boxes.
