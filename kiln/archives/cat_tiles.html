Maneki Neko

$20 * 6" tall by 4" wide. Another translation... Maneki Neko is the "beckoning cat" of Japanese legend. This cat saved the life of a traveling samuri and became a hero. Today you'll see beckoning nekos at many shops and homes in Japan. Search the term here in Google and you'll find tons of links about the icon.
