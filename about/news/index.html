We wish you a Merry Christmas and Happy Hanukkah

Mudcat Pottery features functional pottery, ceramic sculpture and tiles by Darlene Yarnetsky. Made with love and pride in the most beautiful rivertown in America, Madison, Indiana!

Holiday Open Houses

For the holiday season we’re aiming to be open most afternoons 1-5p.m., 1-9 on Fridays and 10-5 on Saturdays. You can also call (812) 265-2445 for an appointment.

Visit us during our city’s holiday homes tour: Step into a winter wonderland during the 27th annual Nights Before Christmas Candlelight Tour of Homes. This spectacular event will be held November 28, 29 & 30 and December 5, 6 & 7 in Historic Madison, Indiana.

ArtJam Christmas in downtown Madison: Friday nights Dec. 12 from 5-9 p.m. ArtJam is the collective art open house for the Downtown Madison Historic Landmark District. Art, music, food and fun— and of course Mudcat Pottery. Sponsored by the Madison Main Street Program, ArtJam is a blast.

Indiana Artisan

Mudcat Pottery is a juried Indiana Artisan!: Darlene’s work was recently juried into the Indiana Artisan project — a state-sponsored program promoting the elite art, fine craft and fine food of Indiana and the communities in which they are made. Come explore the best of Indiana Artisan!

Additional Indiana Artisans from Madison include The Thomas Family Winery, photographer Matt Hicks available at the Village Lights Bookstore, acoustic guitar maker Cliff Bear available at Crawdaddy Music, and Larry Rudolech at the Madison Arts Club gallery.
