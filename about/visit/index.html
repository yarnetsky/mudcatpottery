Our studio is located just two blocks from where the riverboats dock on the Ohio River. The 160-year-old three-story commercial building has been a coffeehouse, a hotel, tenements and a feed store.

The Mudcat studio is located at 301 Jefferson St. in historic downtown Madison, Indiana (the most beautiful rivertown in America if you haven’t heard).
